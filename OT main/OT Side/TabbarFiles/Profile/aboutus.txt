TheraSync is an early-stage prototype application designed to support
Occupational Therapists and individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
through a structured, intuitive, and highly personalized therapeutic workflow.

Our mission is to bridge the communication and coordination gap between
therapists, caregivers, and patients by providing a modern, reliable, and
easy-to-use digital platform. TheraSync focuses on simplifying assessment
processes, recording developmental progress, and helping therapists deliver
more effective and consistent therapy sessions.

As we continue developing TheraSync, we aim to integrate advanced tools,
data-driven insights, and accessible interfaces to make therapy smoother,
more engaging, and more meaningful for every user involved.

TheraSync is in its prototypical phase, and we value all feedback as we build
towards a comprehensive, full-fledged occupational therapy companion platform.
